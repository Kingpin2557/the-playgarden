Ambient sound files (mp3), generated with ElevenLabs.

  music.mp3          - soft background music, loops the whole time (low volume)
  rain.mp3           - rain loop; also used as the bed during a thunderstorm
  snow.mp3           - snowy wind loop, played when it is snowing
  thunder.mp3        - short thunder clap (one-shot)
  thunder_strike.mp3 - second thunder clap; picked at random with thunder.mp3

The looping beds are chosen in src/hooks/useWeatherAudio.ts.
The thunder claps fire on each lightning flash from src/lib/thunderAudio.ts.
